# SaaS Pricing — playbook

## Anchor + decoy + charm

- Show the **Normalpreis** crossed out next to the launch price → reference anchor
- Add a **decoy** (the obvious-bad tier) so the featured tier looks like a deal
- Use **charm pricing** (€197 / €297 / €597) — psychological floor under round numbers

## Three-tier default

| Tier | Mechanic | When |
|---|---|---|
| Solo / starter | One-time fee, low entry | Cold leads who want to try |
| **Featured** (Pro) | One-time + monthly | Highlighted, most clicked |
| Done-for-you | Premium tier with onboarding | High-touch buyers |

The featured tier should be ~2× the cheap one and ~½ the premium one.

## Launch mechanics

- **Scarcity**: "Nur 10 Plätze" — real cap. Track in DB. Stop showing when 0.
- **Time** anchor: "Launch-Aktion bis 31. Juli" — date triggered.
- After cap → switch to Normalpreis automatically. Never lie post-cap.

## Annual prepayment

- 2 months free (16.6% discount) is the classic.
- Surface savings number explicitly: "Sparen €X / Jahr".

## Free trial vs money-back

- **Free trial** wins for tools (low decision risk).
- **Money-back** wins for high-ticket services (commitment + bias toward keeping).
- Don't do both — picks one signal.

## When to use

For every pricing page redesign or A/B test.

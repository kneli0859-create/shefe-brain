# SEO German — playbook

## Keyword research

- Use Google Trends with region=DE
- Look at "Verwandte Anfragen" on the SERP — that's user intent leaked
- B2B keywords: include `B2B`, `Firma`, `Unternehmen`, `gewerblich`
- Long-tail wins faster: "Bürореinigung München Preis Stunde"

## On-page

- `<html lang="de">` always
- `<meta name="description">` 150-160 chars
- Open Graph `og:locale="de_DE"`
- Structured data: LocalBusiness + Service + FAQ schemas
- One H1 per page

## Content patterns that rank in DACH

- "Preisrechner" pages with real interactive calculation
- "Vergleich" pages (X vs Y) — Germans love comparison
- "Erfahrungsbericht" + "Test" pages
- Long-form (1500+ words) with explicit FAQ section

## Avoid

- Translated US content with American examples
- Made-up Umlauts ("ueber" instead of "über") — bad signal
- Keyword stuffing — Google DE is aggressive on this

## Technical

- Sitemap submitted via Search Console
- robots.txt allows crawl, disallows /admin/, /api/
- Page speed: LCP < 2.5s on 4G

## When to use

Any new page being published in German market.

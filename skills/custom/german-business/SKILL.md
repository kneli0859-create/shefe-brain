# German Business — quick reference

## Legal foundations

- **Gewerbeanmeldung** required before first paid sale of products/services.
- **§19 UStG (Kleinunternehmerregelung)** — turnover < €25 000 prev. year and < €100 000 expected this year → no MwSt charged.
- **TMG §5** — Impressum mandatory on every commercial website. Must contain: full name, address (no PO box), Telefon, email, USt-IdNr if relevant.
- **DSGVO** — Datenschutzerklärung mandatory wherever PII is collected. Lawful basis must be named.

## Invoicing rules (Rechnung)

Mandatory fields:
- Vollständiger Name + Anschrift of issuer and recipient
- Steuernummer or USt-IdNr
- Rechnungsdatum + Rechnungsnummer (continuous)
- Leistungsdatum (or period)
- Menge + Art (Beschreibung) der Leistung
- Nettobetrag, Steuersatz, Steuerbetrag, Bruttobetrag
- If §19 UStG: "Gemäß §19 UStG wird keine Umsatzsteuer berechnet."

Retention: 10 years.

## Customer etiquette

- **B2B** → **Sie**, formal. Address by surname. Email signature with full Impressum lines.
- **B2C casual** → still mostly Sie unless explicitly invited to Du.
- Mid-week mornings highest open-rate for B2B email (Tue/Wed/Thu 09:00-10:30).

## Marketing rules

- Cold B2C calls **forbidden** (UWG).
- Email marketing only with explicit prior consent (double-opt-in).
- Cookie consent banner mandatory before non-essential cookies.

## Common gotchas

- Bürgergeld + first paid customer → income must be reported within 7 days.
- Stripe live keys require Gewerbe + bank account in legal entity's name.
- Trial accounts on Stripe **count as Geschäftstätigkeit** in some interpretations — talk to a Steuerberater.

## When to use this skill

When writing landing copy, contracts, AGB/Datenschutz, pricing pages, or planning a launch in the DACH market.

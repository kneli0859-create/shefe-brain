# Strategy: EMA Stack Trend

**Timeframe:** 1h / 4h
**Class:** Trend / swing
**Target win rate:** 45-55% (R:R compensates)

## Indicators
- EMA(9), EMA(21), EMA(50), EMA(200)

## Setup
- All EMAs aligned (9 > 21 > 50 > 200 long; reverse for short)
- Pullback to EMA21 without breaking EMA50

## Entry
- Long: confirmation candle close back above EMA21 with volume
- Short: mirror

## Risk
- SL: below EMA50 (long) / above EMA50 (short)
- TP: trail with EMA21 — exit on close back through 21

## Position sizing
2% account risk per trade.

## Filters
- ADX > 20 (trend strength)
- Avoid earnings/macro events
